Lone Star Map: NYC

Because looking for a bar on Yelp doesn't tell you the one thing you really want to know.

I shot an email to the nice people at Pabst about where I might find a grocery store in Greenpoint that carried Lone Star. They shot back with every business in the whole city that had ordered it within the last 90 days. (As of 3.22.17). A bit of data manipulation and a little Google Maps later, and here we are.

Please feel free to help keep locations up to date. GitHub can be found here.
